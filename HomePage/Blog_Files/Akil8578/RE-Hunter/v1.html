The Royal Enfield Hunter 350 is a modern retro roadster designed for urban riding with a blend of agility, style, and performance. Built on Royal Enfield’s J-series platform, it carries a 349cc, air-oil cooled single-cylinder engine that produces around 20.2 bhp and 27 Nm of torque, paired with a 5-speed gearbox.

Unlike its siblings, the Hunter is lighter, shorter, and more compact, making it one of the most accessible and nimble motorcycles in the Royal Enfield lineup. It features a sporty stance, minimal bodywork, and wide handlebars, delivering excellent maneuverability for city commutes while maintaining highway stability.

The bike comes in two variants – Retro and Metro – offering options in spoke or alloy wheels, different color schemes, and modern touches like a semi-digital instrument cluster with Tripper navigation (on select models).

The Hunter 350 is crafted to appeal to younger riders and city dwellers, combining Royal Enfield’s classic DNA with contemporary urban character, making it a stylish yet practical choice for everyday rides.
