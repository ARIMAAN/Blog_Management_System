Introduction

Dark is a German science fiction thriller that explores the intertwined lives of four families in the small town of Winden. The story begins with the mysterious disappearance of two children, but soon evolves into a complex narrative involving time travel, hidden secrets, and generational trauma.

Themes

  • Time Travel & Fate: The series examines the concept of cyclical time, where the past, present, and future are deeply connected.
  • Family & Secrets: Each family in Winden hides dark truths that fuel the chain of events.
  • Free Will vs. Determinism: Characters constantly struggle with whether they can change destiny or if everything is already predetermined.
  • Duality: Good vs. evil, light vs. dark, and the mirrored lives of characters highlight the constant duality of existence.
